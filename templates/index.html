Research Study

Developing and testing online activities for undergraduate statistics

by Nahuel Melgarejo

supervised by Dr. Herbert

Department of Psychology

2023

This research project aims to investigate the effectiveness of different types of content in educating participants on core statistical concepts. The study will focus on concepts that are taught in 2000-level statistics courses at York University:

  • Central Tendency
  • Variability

Participants will be asked to complete a pre-survey to gauge their confidence in their statistics knowledge before being randomly assigned to one of two groups - Group A or Group B.

After viewing the content, participants will take a post-survey to evaluate their understanding of the statistical concepts that were explained and presented.
