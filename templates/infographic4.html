Chapter 2: Variability

But not all data is the same! Different groups or different populations can and often do differ from each other. Look at the infographic below to learn more.

Variability, also known as dispersion, refers to how spread out or closely clustered a set of values are in a data set.

There are several ways to measure this but we'll focus on three main ones:

i) Range

ii) Variance

iii) Standard Deviation
